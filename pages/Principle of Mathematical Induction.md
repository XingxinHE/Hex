# 📝Definition
Let $P(n)$ be any property pertaining to a natural number $n$. Suppose that $P(0)$ is true, and suppose that whenever $P(n)$ is true, $P(n\mathbin{++})$ is also true. Then $P(n)$ is true for every [[natural number]] $n$.
