# Riemann Hypothesis
- 📝Definition
    - The function
        - $$
          Li(x)=\int _{2}^{x} \frac{dt}{\ln (t)}
          $$
        
    - approximates the number of [[Prime Number]]s smaller than $x$ and is related to what is known as the Riemann Hypothesis.
