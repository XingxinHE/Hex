# 📝Definition


Suppose that $F(x, y)$ is [[differentiable]] and that the equation $F(x, y) = 0$ defines $y$ as a differentiable function of $x$. Then at any point where $F_y \neq 0$,
$$
\frac{dy}{dx}=-\frac{F_x}{F_y}.
$$
