# 📝Definition
- For any subset $S\sub\mathbb{R}^n$, its [[Convex]] hull $conv(S)$ is the smallest [[Convex Set]] containing $S$, or equivalently, the intersection of all [[Convex Set]] containing $S$.

- 📌example of a convex hull
