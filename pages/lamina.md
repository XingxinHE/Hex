- # 🍴Quick Digest -> lamina
	- ## 📝Definition
	  A two-dimensional planar closed surface $L$ which has a mass $M$ and a surface density $\sigma(x,y)$ (in units of mass per areas squared) such that
	  $$
	  M=\int_{L}\sigma(x,y)dxdy. 
	  $$
