$$
\alpha(I)\subset P
$$
where $I$ is an open interval in the [[smooth curve#ğŸ“Definition]].

> [!info] Remark
> A plane curve can be always transformed by a rigid motion to $P=\{z=0\}$ and its parameterized equation can be simplified as $\alpha(t)=(x(t), y(t), 0)$.
