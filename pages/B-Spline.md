## 🔴degree
**📝Definition**
Suppose
- $N_{\text{degree}}$ denotes the degree of a B-Spline,
- $N_{\text{control points}}$ denotes the number of control points,
then we have this relationship:
$$
N_{\text{degree}}=[1,N_{\text{control points}}-1].
