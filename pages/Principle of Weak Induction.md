# 📝Definition
Let $D$ be a subset of the nonnegative integers $\mathbb{Z}^*$ with the properties that
(1) the [[integer]] $0$ is in $D$
(2) any time that $n$ is in $D$,
one can show that $n+1$ is also in $D$. Under these conditions, $D=\mathbb{Z}^*$.
