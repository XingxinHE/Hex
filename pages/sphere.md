# ✒Notation
A sphere with center at $P_0(x_0,y_0,z_0)$ and with radius $a$ can be expressed as:
$$
\begin{align}
(x-x_0)^2+(y-y_0)^2+(z-z_0)^2=a^2.
\end{align}
$$
> [!info] Remark
> Distance between a point $P(x,y,z)$ lies on the sphere and $P_0$ is exactly $|P_0 P|=a$.
