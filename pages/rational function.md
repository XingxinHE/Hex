alias:: rational polynomial function

- 📝Definition
	- A quotient of two [[polynomial]]s $P(z)$ and $Q(z)$,
		- $$
		  R(z)\equiv\frac{P(z)}{Q(z)}
		  $$
	- is called a rational function, or sometimes a rational polynomial function.
