alias:: Local Minima

- ## 📝Definition
	- $f$ has a local minimum at $x=a$ if $f(a)\leq f(x)$ for all $x$ "near $a$" (within some small distance of $a$).
	- A point $x^∗$ is a local minimizer if there is a neighborhood $N$ of $x^∗$ such that $f(x^∗) ≤ f(x)$for all $x ∈ N$.
