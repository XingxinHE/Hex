$$
H(x)=\int _{0}^{x} h(t)\,  dt \qquad \text{where} h(t)=\begin{cases}  1 &  \text{if } t=0 \\ \frac{\sin (t)}{t} &  \text{if } t\neq 0 \end{cases}
$$

- Recall that the integrand $h(t)$ is continuous since $\lim _{t\rightarrow 0} \frac{\sin (t)}{t} =1$.
